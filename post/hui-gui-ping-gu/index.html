回归评估

2020-08-11

  1. 对于mmm个样本(x1,y1),(x2,y2),....,(xm,ym)(x_1,y_1),(x_2,y_2),....,(x_m,y_m)(x1​,y1​),(x2​,y2​),....,(xm​,ym​)
    模型的预估计值为(x1,y11),(x2,y22),....,(xm,ymm))(x_1,{y_1}^{1}),(x_2,{y_2}^2),....,(x_m,{y_m}^m))(x1​,y1​1),(x2​,y2​2),....,(xm​,ym​m))

  2. 计算样本的总平方和TSS(Total Sum of Squares)
    TSS = ∑i=1m(yi−y−)\sum\limits_{i=1}^m(y_i-y^-)i=1∑m​(yi​−y−)

  3. 样本的伪方差
    Var(Y) = TSSm\frac{TSS}{m}mTSS​

  4. 计算残差平方和 RSS(Residual Sum of Squares)
    RSS = ∑i=1m(yii−yi)\sum\limits_{i=1}^m(y_i^i-y_i)i=1∑m​(yii​−yi​)

  5. R2=1−RSSTSSR_2 = 1-\frac{RSS}{TSS}R2​=1−TSSRSS​
    R2R_2R2​ 越大，拟合效果越好，R2R_2R2​的最优值为1，若模型预测为随机值，R2R_2R2​有可能为负值
    若预测值为样本期望值，则R2R_2R2​为0

  6. ESS(Explained Sum of Squares)
    ESS=∑i=0m(yi−y−)ESS = \sum\limits_{i=0}^m(y^i-y^-)ESS=i=0∑m​(yi−y−)
    TSS = ESS + RSS
    只有在无偏估计时上述等式成立，否则TSS≥ESS+RSSTSS \geq ESS + RSSTSS≥ESS+RSS
    ESS 又称为回归平方和SSR
